Trust and Indoctrination

You and 99 other people are in a room together
You know they are there but you can't see or talk to them
Now into the room walks 2 people
These 2 people are there to present their own truths to everyone
but they break it up into sections
So one of them will talk to you and 49 other people first
while the other person will talk to the other 50
After each person is done speaking to their people
they will switch and present their truth to the other group
Both of these people are presenting you with a topic you know absolutley
nothing about meaning you have never
heard of it and have no previous understanding of it. Now the question is.
Who do you trust?
Which truth will you choose to believe?
