About

Anthony Yermolov

Welcome to the TruView Podcast and thank you for stopping by the About Us page!
My name is Anthony Yermolov, and I am the host of this podcast. I am a senior at
Penn State Abington and will be graduating spring 2023 with a Bachleor of Science
in Finance and a Minor in Entrepreneurship and Innovation. In my free time, I enjoy
to play baseball, go bowling, play chess, and hang out with friends. My passion for
entrepeneurship and sharing eye opening thoughts is what inspired me to start this
podcast. My goal with The TruView Podcast is to present my thoughts and learn as
much as I can while speaking on different topics. I have always loved doing things
for myself and throwing myself into projects. Because of this, I will be working hard
to accomplish the goal I have set for myself. On this podcast, you can expect views
and opinions from regular people. I will be discussing relevant world topics and
experiences that I have gone through and will be inviting guests to share their views
on these topics. I am committed to bringing you the best content possible
(even though it is not great yet), and am excited to see the improvment I will make on
this journey.
